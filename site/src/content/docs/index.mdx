## Features

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Declarative API" icon="pencil">
    Build scenes with `Shape`, `Group`, and `Path` composables. Transforms accumulate automatically through the hierarchy.
  </Card>
  <Card title="High Performance" icon="rocket">
    Per-node dirty tracking, prepared-scene caching, and spatial indexing. Only changed subtrees re-render.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Interactive" icon="approve-check-circle">
    Built-in tap and drag gestures with spatial hit testing. Camera pan and zoom with `CameraState`.
  </Card>
  <Card title="6 Built-in Shapes" icon="puzzle">
    Prism, Pyramid, Cylinder, Octahedron, Stairs, and Knot — plus custom shape support via `Shape.extrude` and `CustomNode`.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
